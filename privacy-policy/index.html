Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Thank you for using Dabby. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the Dabby app, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By using Dabby, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Dabby is a simple timer application and does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or location.

However, Dabby uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language settings
  • App usage data and interaction events
  • IP address (used to estimate general location for ad targeting)
  • Diagnostic and crash data

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within the app (via Google AdMob)
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Improve app stability and diagnose technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to any third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

3. Third-Party Services

Dabby uses the following third-party service:

Google AdMob

https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

4. Data Retention

Dabby does not store any user data on its own servers. Any data collected by third-party services such as Google AdMob is retained according to their respective privacy policies and data retention schedules.

5. Children's Privacy

Dabby is not intended for use by children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.
